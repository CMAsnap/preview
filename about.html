About CMAsnap®

CMAsnap was built for real estate pros who don’t have time to wrestle with clunky tools or settle for vague estimates.

Our mission is simple

Make creating Comparative Market Analyses (CMAs) faster, clearer, and easier — so you can turn insights into action and conversations into closings.

We believe agents deserve better than spreadsheets, generic valuation models, or bloated software designed for someone else.

CMAsnap gives you what you actually need:

  • A streamlined process to build detailed, visual CMAs in minutes
  • Flexibility to adjust comps and pricing strategies on the fly
  • A client-ready report that supports your expertise, not just an algorithm

Why We Built it

CMAsnap was created by industry veterans who’ve worked alongside agents, not just sold to them. We saw the same problem again and again:

Agents spending hours building reports that clients skimmed in seconds.

So we designed a better way. It’s clear, customizable, and purpose-built for real-world real estate work.

Privately Owned, Agent-Focused

We’re a small, independent team — no outside investors, no corporate red tape. That means we answer to our customers, not a boardroom.

Every update, feature, and workflow is shaped by feedback from the agents and brokers who use CMAsnap every day.

We’re just getting started. Whether you’re a top producer or building your business, CMAsnap is here to support your success — with tools that respect your time and showcase your value.
